System Status

System Login Issues (11:30am - Jan. 29, 2020):

Around 11:30am, IS&T received several reports that the users were having trouble logging into their accounts for Blackboard Learn and my.chapman.edu. IS&T was able to identify and resolve the issue. All login services were successfully restored and confirmed operational by 12pm. If you continue to experience issues logging into your Chapman University accounts, please contact the IS&T Service Desk at Servicedesk@chapman.edu or (714) 997-6600).

Zynga Data Breach - December 19th
 
We have learned of a data breach affecting Zynga, the company that makes the popular game, Words With Friends, as outlined in this article: https://www.theverge.com/2019/12/19/21029682/zynga-hack-words-with-friends-draw-something-password-data-breach If you are have an account with Zynga or Words With Friends, we strongly recommend you to change your password immediately. If you have any questions, please contact our Information Security team at infosec@chapman.edu.
