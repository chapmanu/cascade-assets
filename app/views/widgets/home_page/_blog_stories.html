The inauguration of Dr. Daniele C. Struppa as Chapman University's thirteenth president welcomes guests from around the globe. This weeklong celebration draws on the treasured traditions and pageantry of higher education through the ages, and represents a formal passing of the torch from Dr. James L. Doti, who served as Chapman's president from 1991 to 2016.
